Son of Mars

My First Short Film - BUAS

“During World War II, the 17-year-old Jürgen who is a part of the Hitler Jugend, is send to fight in the war. He’s unexperienced and scared, especially when he gets ambushed in the forest, by the American soldier Michael. In shock by the young age of Jürgen and the war-trauma, Michael loses his mind, triggering Jürgen to attack. Will he become a Son of Mars?”
